## What is Moroccan Argan Oil?

Moroccan argan oil is a natural oil that comes from the kernels of the argan tree. This tree grows in the dry deserts of Morocco, North Africa. The oil is made by pressing the argan kernels to get the liquid inside. Pure argan oil is golden yellow and has a light, pleasant smell. It is 100% natural and does not have any chemicals added to it.

The best argan oil is cold-pressed. This means the oil is made without heat, so all the good nutrients stay inside. Cold-pressed argan oil is more expensive, but it is worth it because it is stronger and better for your skin and hair.

A lot of tourists ask me how to tell real argan oil from fake when they're shopping in Morocco. Usually the giveaway is the price and how it pours: real cold-pressed oil is never dirt cheap, and it moves slowly, not thin and watery like sunflower oil mixed in to stretch the bottle.
