Amouage by Surf Maroc is a stylish oceanfront boutique hotel in Taghazout made for travelers who want to combine surfing, yoga, and relaxed coastal living. It suits surfers, couples, and solo visitors who prefer a social surf vibe over a large traditional resort. Read our review before booking. [Taghazout](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Taghazout) has become a magnet for surfers from around the world.

![Woman in a dress stands on a wooden deck, gazing at an ocean sunset in taghazout morocco from amouage hotel. Cacti frame the scene, creating a serene, warm atmosphere.](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/b51d6b_3579d44aafb64935954344a632c95557~mv2.webp)

If you are planning a trip to Taghazout and want a stay that mixes comfort with surf culture, Amouage by Surf Maroc is one of the most talked-about options.

This boutique oceanfront hotel has built a strong reputation among surfers, couples, and digital nomads who want a relaxed but stylish place to stay.

In this detailed review, you will learn what makes Amouage special, what tourists can enjoy there, and if it is the right choice for your Morocco trip before you book it.

## Location: Right Above Anchor Point

Amouage sits in the heart of Taghazout, about 30 minutes north of Agadir. The hotel overlooks the famous Anchor Point, one of Morocco’s best surf breaks.

**Why the location works well:**

1. Walking distance to Taghazout cafés and restaurants
1. Direct ocean views from many rooms
1. Easy access to top surf spots
1. Close enough to Agadir airport for simple transfers

For surfers especially, the location is hard to beat.

Amouage is a boutique hotel, not a large resort. The design follows a clean, modern style with soft neutral colors and Moroccan touches.

**Room categories include:**

- Garden rooms
- Ocean rooms
- Ocean balcony rooms
- Spacious suites

**What guests usually like:**

- Comfortable beds
- Strong natural light
- Simple but tasteful decor
- Many rooms with Atlantic views

Ocean balcony rooms are usually the best value if you want the full Taghazout experience. The view from there is genuinely impressive.

## What You Can Enjoy at Amouage Hotel by Surf Maroc

This is where Amouage really stands out. It is not just a place to sleep, it is a lifestyle stay.

### Surf and Surf Classes

Because the hotel is run by Surf Maroc, surf, obviously, is the main focus.

This means you can enjoy:

1. Daily surf lessons for beginners
1. Guided surf trips to different breaks
1. Board and wetsuit rental
1. Video coaching for intermediate surfers
1. Easy access to Anchor Point

Even if you have never surfed before, this is one of the easiest places in Morocco to start learning surf. You'll also find surf gurus everywhere to help you.

### Yoga with ocean views

Amouage also attracts many wellness travelers. If you are interested in yoga alongside surf, this is simply the right hotel for you.

What to expect:

1. Rooftop yoga shala
1. Daily yoga classes
1. Retreat weeks during the year
1. Calm sunset sessions
1. A very relaxing atmosphere overall

This makes Amouage hotel popular with solo female travelers and couples looking for a balanced surf-and-relax trip.

### Pool, spa, and relaxation

When you are not surfing, or doing yoga, you can also enjoy:

- Infinity pool facing the ocean
- Small spa and massage treatments
- Sun terrace
- Gym area
- Restaurant and bar

The atmosphere is social but still relaxed, not a party hotel.

From what I've seen, Taghazout's surf scene has grown a lot over the past several years, and boutique hotels like this one are part of why the village feels noticeably more polished than it did not long ago.

### Food and dining

The hotel restaurant serves:

- Healthy breakfast options
- Moroccan dishes
- International plates
- Fresh juices and coffee
- Evening bar drinks

Many guests like the terrace dining with sunset views. However, since Taghazout village is small, you can also easily walk to local restaurants for cheaper meals.

## Prices Range at Amouage Hotel

Amouage is mid-to-upper range for Taghazout village.

**Approximate nightly prices**:

- Standard rooms: from ~1500 MAD
- Ocean rooms: from ~2200 MAD
- Balcony rooms: from ~2700 MAD

Prices change a lot in peak surf season (October–March). Prices also goes high during summer because of the high demand.

## Pros and Cons of Amouage Hotel

Just like any hotel in the world, Amouage by Surf Maroc has also some drawbacks that you need to be aware of. Let's start by the advantages and then see the disadvantages.

### Pros: Why staying at Amouage

- Prime oceanfront location
- Strong surf programs
- Stylish boutique feel
- Good for solo travelers
- Walkable to village center
- Beautiful sunset views

### Cons: Why Amouage may not suit you

- Not a full 5-star luxury resort
- Can feel social/busy in peak season
- Limited parking
- Prices higher than many local guesthouses (this is reasonable because the hotel offers more)

## So, Who Should Stay at Amouage?

- Surfers (all levels)
- Couples
- Solo travelers
- Yoga and wellness fans
- Digital nomads

**Maybe not ideal for:**

- Large families with kids

## How to Reach Taghazout and Amouage

If you are planning your trip to Taghazout, reaching the village, and then Amouage by Surf Maroc,  is quite simple. Below is a clear step-by-step guide starting from your home country and then from Agadir.

### Step 1: From Your Home Country to Morocco

Most international travelers arrive via Agadir–Al Massira Airport, which is the closest airport to Taghazout (about 45–60 minutes away). Your typical route will be like this:

1. Fly to Agadir–Al Massira Airport
1. Transfer to Agadir city
1. Continue north to Taghazout
1. Take a short ride to Amouage hotel

If flights to Agadir are expensive, some travelers fly to Marrakech and then travel by bus or car (about 3–4 hours to Taghazout).

### Step 2: From Agadir to Taghazout

The distance from Agadir to Taghazout is about 20 km (30 minutes). You have several transport options depending on your budget and comfort level.

#### Bus: Cheapest but can be crowded

![White city bus on a sunny street with palm trees and mountains in the background. LED sign reads '08 M&#039;Dokkara-Taddart.'](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/b51d6b_692ad4c43a624ea0a9875e72e0b18be9~mv2.jpg)

If you want the cheapest way to reach **Taghazout** from **Agadir**, you can take the public buses run by **Alsa**. You will pay very little, and the trip usually takes 30–45 minutes. However, be ready for crowds in summer and limited space for luggage. Choose this option if you travel light and want to save money.

- Price: very cheap
- Travel time: 30–45 minutes
- Best for: budget travelers
- Public buses run regularly between Agadir and Taghazout. However:
- In summer and weekends, buses can become very crowded.
- Limited space for surfboards or large luggage.
- Comfort level is basic.

Good if you travel light and want to save money.

### Grand Taxi: Cheap and practical

![Yellow and white taxis parked under shady trees in a lot, with people walking nearby. Buildings and street signs are visible in the background.](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/b51d6b_46116ed1b0fd475699bb15ac931044df~mv2.jpg)

You can also take a grand taxi, which is a common and affordable choice. The ride takes about 30 minutes, and you usually leave once the car fills up. Before you get in, make sure you agree on the price and try to pick a modern, well-maintained vehicle for better comfort, especially if you have bags or a surfboard.

- Price: cheap to moderate
- Travel time: ~30 minutes
- Best for: travelers who want flexibility

Grand taxis leave from Agadir taxi stations once full. Follow these Important tips before taking a taxi to Taghazout:

1. Choose modern vehicles when possible
1. Agree on the price before departure
1. If you have luggage or a surfboard, consider paying for extra seats

A grand taxi is a solid budget-friendly option with more comfort than the bus.

#### inDrive: (similar to Uber) Often the best balance

![White car with 'inDrive' logo driving on road, green trees in background. Text: 'Set your Fare.' Bright, sunny setting.](https://static.wixstatic.com/media/b51d6b_e7d33eac13884fe2aba9d02a43d1de8f~mv2.webp)

If you prefer door-to-door service, you can use **inDrive**. You simply request a ride in the app and negotiate the price with the driver. This option saves you time, avoids waiting for taxis to fill, and usually gives you a cleaner, more comfortable car. For many travelers, this is the easiest everyday choice. - Price: moderate but fair
- Travel time: ~30 minutes
- Best for: comfort + price control

Using inDrive in Agadir has become very popular with tourists, and this is why many travelers prefer it:

- You negotiate the price in the app
- Door-to-door service
- Usually newer, cleaner cars
- No waiting for the taxi to fill
- Good for late arrivals

For many visitors, this is the best overall option from Agadir to Amouage.

- Price: moderate to high
- Travel time: ~30 minutes from Agadir
- Best for: travelers who want a pre-booked, authorized transfer

Another strong option is GetTransfer.com. Many tourists prefer it because it works with licensed drivers and allows you to arrange your ride in advance.

**Why this can be even better than inDrive:**

- Authorized and pre-arranged service
- You book before arrival (less stress at the airport)
- Fixed price agreed in advance
- Good vehicle selection (including larger cars for surfboards)
- Driver meets you at the airport or hotel and take you directly to Taghazout or Amouage hotel doors.

### Bottom Line: How To Get to Taghazout & Amouage

If you want the smoothest and most secure arrival to Amouage by Surf Maroc, [<u>**GetTransfer**</u>](https://gettransfer.tpk.ro/XWrdiGm7) is often the most comfortable choice, while [<u>**inDrive**</u>](https://indrive.tpk.ro/No9sxnjG) remains the best balance between price and convenience.

If you're on a budget, consider a modern grand taxi first. The bus should be your last option, especially in summer.

#### Final Tips for Reaching Amouage Hotel

1. Save the hotel location on Google Maps before arrival
1. If arriving late at night, avoid the bus and use inDrive or a taxi
1. If you carry a surfboard, confirm space with the driver
1. The hotel is inside Taghazout village, not Taghazout Bay resort area

## Final verdict: Is Amouage Hotel Worth It

If your goal in Taghazout is to surf, relax, and enjoy ocean views in a stylish setting, Amouage by Surf Maroc is one of the strongest boutique choices in the village. It successfully mixes Moroccan coastal charm with a modern surf lifestyle.

For many tourists, especially first-time visitors to Taghazout, it offers the right balance between comfort, location, and authentic surf atmosphere. So, yes. Amouage hotel is a great choice, unless you're looking for cheaper and regular guesthouses or if you're a family with kids.
