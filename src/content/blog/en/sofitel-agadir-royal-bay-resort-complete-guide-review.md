Sofitel Agadir Royal Bay Resort is a good 5-star hotel located in the tourist area of Agadir, close to the beach. It is known for its mix of Moroccan design and modern comfort. 

In this guide, you will find everything you need to know about this hotel, including rooms, facilities, location, and what to expect during your stay. [Agadir](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agadir) has grown into one of Morocco's most popular beach destinations.

As a local, I have watched Agadir grow into one of the country's busiest beach destinations over the past decade, and Founty, where this hotel sits, is right in the middle of that growth.

## What is Sofitel Agadir Royal Bay Resort?

Sofitel Agadir Royal Bay Resort is a luxury beachfront hotel designed in a style inspired by [<u>Moroccan riads</u>](/post/morocco-riad-list-the-best-riads-in-morocco). The hotel has gardens, palm trees, and open spaces that give a calm and relaxing atmosphere.

It reopened after renovation in 2024 and offers modern comfort while keeping a traditional Moroccan look.

This hotel is a good choice if you want comfort, quiet, and easy access to the beach.

It's important to know that there are two Sofitel hotels next to each other in Agadir. Both hotels are nice and worth booking. Each one offers a slightly different atmosphere. This post is mainly about [<u>**Sofitel  Royal Bay Resort**</u>](https://booking.tpk.ro/RwJiG8BL)** **which offers a Moroccan touch to your stay. 

If you want a more modern luxurious touch, you can then book [<u>**Sofitel Thalassa**</u>](https://booking.tpk.ro/rFGs4SYx)<u>**.**</u>

## Sofitel Agadir Royal Bay vs Sofitel Thalassa: What’s the Difference?

Many travelers do not know that there are **two Sofitel hotels in Agadir located next to each other**: Sofitel Agadir Royal Bay Resort and Sofitel Agadir Thalassa Sea & Spa.

Even if they share the same brand and beachfront location, they offer **different styles and experiences**. One focuses more on a classic resort atmosphere, while the other is more about wellness and spa.

Here is a simple comparison to help you choose the right one for your stay:

If you are looking for a **Moroccan-style resort with more activity**, go for **Royal Bay Resort**.If your goal is **relaxation, spa, and quiet time**, choose **Thalassa Sea & Spa**.

## Location and How to Get to Sofitel Hotel from The Airport

The hotel is located in the Founty tourist area of Agadir, near the main beach.

- About 20–30 minutes from Agadir Al Massira Airport
- Around 5–10 minutes drive to Agadir Marina
- Walking distance to the beach

The location is convenient if you want both relaxation and access to the city. You have several options to reach the hotel, depending on your budget and comfort level.

### 2. Airport Taxi (Simple and Available)

Taxis are available outside the airport at any time.

- Travel time: about 20–30 minutes
- You pay directly to the driver

From traveler experiences, the price is usually around **200–300 MAD**, depending on the time and negotiation.

**Things to keep in mind:**

- Always agree on the price before starting
- Some drivers may not speak English
- Payment is usually in cash

### 3. Shuttle Transfers (Budget Option)

Some platforms like [<u>**Intui.travel**</u>](https://intui.tpk.ro/AScgvwWU) and[<u>** HolidayTaxis**</u>](https://holidaytaxis.tpk.ro/yvUlf3uU) offer **shared shuttle services**.

This option is cheaper than private transfers, but:

- You may stop at other hotels
- Travel time can be longer

It is a good option if you want to save money and don’t mind waiting.

### 4. Public Transport (Lowest Cost)

Public transport is possible, but not the easiest option.

You may need to:

- Take a bus from the airport
- Then a taxi to the hotel

Buses do not always run frequently, especially at night.

This option is better for budget travelers who already know the area.

### 5. Car Rental (More Freedom)

You can also rent a car at the airport.

This is a good option if you plan to:

- Visit Taghazout
- Explore Paradise Valley
- Travel outside Agadir

However, for a short stay, a transfer is usually easier.

### Which Option Should You Choose?

- **Best overall:** Private transfer (easy and stress-free)
- **Best budget:** Taxi or shuttle
- **Best for flexibility:** Car rental

If you arrive late, travel with family, or carry luggage, booking a transfer in advance is usually the best choice.

## What to Do Nearby

Agadir is a good destination for both relaxing and exploring.

Near the hotel, you can:

- Walk along Agadir beach
- Visit Agadir Oufella for a city view
- Explore Souk El Had market
- Try water sports like surfing

Agadir has more than 300 days of sunshine, so you can visit at any time of the year. You can also visit nearby towns for more fun. 

**Local Tip:** Souk El Had is busiest, and most interesting, in the late afternoon once all the stalls are fully set up and the midday heat has eased off. Going right at opening means a lot of half-empty stalls.

## Day Trips and Nearby Places

### Taghazout (Surf and Relaxation)

Taghazout is a small fishing village about **20 km north of Agadir**. Today, it is famous for surfing and its relaxed atmosphere.

You can:

- Take a surf lesson
- Sit in a café by the ocean
- Eat fresh seafood
- Enjoy a calm day away from the city

Even if you do not surf, it is still worth visiting for the views and the atmosphere.

### Paradise Valley (Nature and Swimming)

Paradise Valley is located in the Atlas Mountains, about **1 hour from Agadir**.

It is known for:

- Natural pools
- Palm trees
- Rocky landscapes

You can swim, walk, and enjoy nature. It is a good option if you want a break from the beach.

### Tamraght (Quiet Alternative to Taghazout)

Tamraght is located between Agadir and Taghazout. It is quieter than Taghazout and less crowded.

It is a good place for:

- Yoga retreats
- Surf lessons
- Calm stays near the ocean

### Imsouane (Peaceful Ocean Views)

Imsouane is about **1.5 hours from Agadir**. It is a small village known for its long waves and peaceful atmosphere.

You can:

- Enjoy quiet beaches
- Watch surfers
- Eat fresh fish near the harbor

This is a good place if you want something very calm and simple.

### Souss Massa National Park (Wildlife and Nature)

Located south of Agadir, this park is known for its wildlife and natural landscapes.

You may see:

- Birds (including rare species)
- Sand dunes
- Empty beaches

It is a good option if you are interested in nature and photography.

### How to Do It

If you stay at Sofitel Agadir Royal Bay Resort, you can easily combine **relaxing at the hotel** with **short trips outside the city**.

- Stay at the hotel in the morning

This way, you enjoy both comfort and local experiences.
