For GITEX Africa, the best area to stay in Marrakech is [<u>**Gueliz**</u>](https://booking.tpk.ro/BvxNqBKj) for easy access to the venue. [<u>**Hivernage**</u>](https://booking.tpk.ro/ajx3QFId) is ideal for luxury stays, while the [<u>**Medina**</u>](https://booking.tpk.ro/hiwUofTf) suits visitors who want a traditional experience and accept a longer commute. [Marrakesh](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Marrakesh) remains one of Morocco's most visited and vibrant cities.

If you plan to attend GITEX Africa, choosing the right place to stay in Marrakech can make your trip much easier. During the event, the city becomes busy, hotel prices rise, and traffic can slow you down. Staying in the right area helps you save time and avoid stress.

GITEX Africa takes place at the **Marrakech Exhibition Center (Route de Casablanca)**, so location matters a lot. Some neighborhoods offer quick access to the venue, while others give you a more traditional Moroccan experience.

This year's edition, GITEX Africa 2026, took place April 7 to 9 at the Marrakech Exhibition Center. The neighborhood advice below applies just as well to future editions of the event, since the venue and the city's layout stay the same year to year.

As a Moroccan who knows Marrakech's neighborhoods well, I'd say the Gueliz vs Hivernage choice for an event like this comes down to whether you want to be near restaurants and everyday life, or somewhere quieter to recover between sessions.

In this guide, you will find the best areas to stay for GITEX Africa visitors, hotel suggestions for different budgets, and practical tips to help you book the right accommodation.

## Best Areas to Stay for GITEX Africa

### 1. Gueliz — Best Overall for GITEX Visitors

Gueliz is the modern heart of Marrakech and one of the most practical areas to stay during GITEX Africa. Built during the French period, this district has wide streets, shopping centers, cafes, and many business-friendly hotels. 

It feels more organized and easier to navigate than the Medina, which is why many professionals prefer it. From Gueliz, you can usually reach the Marrakech Exhibition Center in about 10 to 15 minutes by taxi, making your daily commute to the event simple and predictable.

**Why stay here:**

- Close to the exhibition center (10–15 minutes by taxi)
- Many business hotels
- Restaurants and cafes nearby
- Easier transportation than the Medina

This is the best option for business travelers and first-time visitors.

### 2. Hivernage — Best for Comfort and Luxury

Hivernage is one of the most upscale neighborhoods in Marrakech and a comfortable choice for visitors attending GITEX Africa. This area is known for its luxury hotels, quiet streets, and clean, well-planned layout. 

Many high-end restaurants, lounges, and conference-friendly hotels are located here, which makes it popular with business travelers. 

From Hivernage, you can usually reach the Marrakech Exhibition Center in about 10 to 15 minutes by taxi, so you stay close to the event while enjoying a more relaxed and polished environment.

**Why stay here:**

- High-end hotels
- Quiet and clean streets
- Good taxi access to the venue
- Close to restaurants and nightlife

Travel time to GITEX venue usually takes about 10 to 15 minutes.

This option is best for professionals and luxury travelers.

### 3. Medina — Best for Cultural Experience

The Medina is the historic heart of Marrakech and the best area if you want a traditional Moroccan atmosphere during your visit to GITEX Africa. This old walled city is famous for its narrow streets, colorful souks, and beautiful riads that offer a unique local experience. 

Staying in the Medina lets you enjoy landmarks like Jemaa el-Fnaa in the evening after the event. However, it is farther from the exhibition center, and taxis cannot always reach your riad door because of its narrow alleys, so plan extra travel time each morning.

**Pros:**

- Unique traditional stays
- Close to major attractions
- Memorable cultural experience

**Cons for GITEX visitors:**

- Farther from the exhibition center
- Taxis may not reach your riad door
- Slower morning travel

Travel time to venue: 20–30 minutes (sometimes more in traffic)

**Best for:** visitors who mix business with tourism

## How to Get to GITEX Africa Venue

**Most visitors use:**

- Petit taxi
- [<u>**inDrive**</u>](https://indrive.tpk.ro/No9sxnjG) (similar to Uber, but you get to choose the price. [<u>**Learn how to use it here**</u>](/post/indrive-alternative-to-uber-and-taxis-in-morocco))
- Hotel shuttle (some hotels offer it, or you can book  a private one at [<u>**GetTransfer.com**</u>](https://gettransfer.tpk.ro/XWrdiGm7))

**Local Tip:** Parking at the exhibition center itself has been a recurring complaint in past editions, with some exhibitors reporting waits of an hour or more to find a spot. Taking a taxi or inDrive instead of driving yourself avoids this entirely.

**Typical travel times:**

- From Gueliz: 10–15 minutes
- From Hivernage: 10–15 minutes
- From Medina: 20–30 minutes

Morning traffic can be heavy during the event, so leave early.

## Important Booking Tips (Many Visitors Miss This)

**1. Book early: **Hotels fill quickly during GITEX Africa. Prices can double close to the event.

**2. Check taxi access: **If you stay in the Medina, make sure taxis can reach nearby.

**3. Prefer flexible cancellation: **Event plans can change.

**4. Stay near main roads: **This saves time every morning.

## Where to Stay in Marrakech for GITEX Africa in a Nutshell

Choosing where to stay in Marrakech for GITEX Africa can make a big difference in your trip. For most visitors, **Gueliz** offers the best balance between convenience and comfort, while **Hivernage** suits luxury travelers and the **Medina** fits those who want a cultural experience.

If you plan to attend GITEX Africa, try to book your hotel early and stay within easy taxi distance of the exhibition center. This simple step will save you time and help you enjoy both the event and the city.
